Brownies

This is the simple guide to making the best 7 ingredient brownies!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup salted butter - melted
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line your pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix sugar + eggs on high for 3–4 minutes.
  3. Add melted butter + vanilla — mix it in.
  4. Add flour, cocoa powder, and salt — fold until it’s a thick batter.
  5. Optional: Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Pour into your pan and bake 33–38 mins
  7. Let cool for 1 hour before cutting.
